Abstract submission

Thank you very much for your interest in the NCC 2020 and submission of Abstract to the conference. Please prepare your abstract for oral and poster presentation as MS Word file, underline the name of the presenting author and send us along the registration form on the following email address: icc2019@icp.edu.pk .The receiving of your abstract will be acknowledged immediately, and you will be informed about the status of your abstract after technical evaluation by peer reviewers.

Key deadlines

Abstract submission opens 15.09.2019
Abstract Submission Deadline 14.02.2020
Abstract decision notification After the technical evaluations
Registration Deadline 01.03.2020

Registration

For registration please fill in the ( registration form ) and send on the following email address:icc2019@icp.edu.pk

Registration Fee*

Participants Fee
Students Pak Rs. 1500/-
Faculty Members/Scientists Pak Rs. 3000/-
Persons from Industries Pak Rs. 4500/-

*Registration fee includes workshop materials, lunch and refreshments during tea/coffee breaks.Registration fee can be paid as demand draft in the name of Chief Organizer of the workshop, Department of Chemistry, Islamia College Peshawar, or online.

Accommodation & Transport

Accommodation upon request will be provided on self payment basis to the delegates in different guest houses of the University and hotels, if deemed necessary. The accommodation will be arranged at preferential rates, particularly for the student participants. The pick and drop from/to accommodation to/from conference venue is included in the complementary services from the organizers.
