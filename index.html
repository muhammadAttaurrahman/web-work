Welcome to National Conference on Chemistry (NCC 2020)

We are pleased to announce the first national conference organized by the Department of Chemistry, Islamia College Peshawar (A public sector University). We formally invite you on behalf of the organizing committee to attend the National Conference on Chemistry (NCC 2020) under the theme “Chemistry for growth and sustainability” which will be held on 18 – 20 March 2020 at the historical Islamia College Peshawar located in Peshawar, best known as the city of flowers. The conference is aimed to make a meeting point for the teachers, researchers, students and other relevant personnel from the chemical sciences who are actively engaged in teaching of and research in Chemistry, and all other related fields like chemical engineering and physics. The NCC 2020 will give an opportunity to the delegates to interact with one another, and share their experiences, knowledge and solutions to the challenges in chemical sciences in a dynamic and serene environment. The technical program includes plenary lectures, invited talks, paper presentations and posters exhibition in the major fields of Chemical Sciences including but not limited to :

  • Physical Chemistry
  • Inorganic Chemistry
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Industrial Chemistry
  • Computational Chemistry
  • Nanomaterials and Nanochemistry
  • Analytical Chemistry
  • Biochemistry
  • Polymer Chemistry
  • Material Chemistry and Physics
  • Chemical Engineering

We are committed to ensure attendance of young researchers, particularly of graduate students by encouraging them through subsidized registration fees and also by providing them accommodations at affordable rates. The NCC 2020 venue is easily accessible through air, roads and rails, and the city of Peshawar and its suburbs are rich in spots to be visited. We are looking forward to welcoming you to the NCC 2020 at Islamia College Peshawar, and we hope that the conference will be a great scientific and social event for all the delegates.
